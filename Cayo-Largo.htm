Moncayo Golf Resort - Puerto Rico

Moncayo(formerly Cayo Largo) is a newly proposed golf resort on a peninsula in Fajardo, Puerto Rico.

Adjacent to the property is the Cabo Rojo Lighthouse, a historic lighthouse on the western tip of Cabo Rojo. Also nearby is Flamenco Beach, a beautiful white-sand beach located on the eastern tip of Cabo Rojo.

The resort property has a tropical climate with warm temperatures/high humidity. Average temperatures range from 75 degrees Fahrenheit (24 degrees Celsius) in winter vs. 85 degrees Fahrenheit (29 degrees Celsius) in summer. Peak season for the resort will be the coolest months of December through February.
