Moncayo Golf Resort - Martin Ebert - Puerto Rico

Moncayo(formerly Cayo Largo) is a newly proposed golf resort on a peninsula in Fajardo, Puerto Rico. The golf course design will be led by Martin Ebert and is part of a 610 acre property that includes Four Seasons hotel with 315 luxury residences. As of July 2023, the property changed hands as reported by El Nuevo Dia. Computer generated imagery(CGI) for the design plan has been produced and approved.

Previously, the project was being developed by Ardent Residential and DLJ Real Estate Capital Partners. The golf course will be set clifftop on the edge of the El Yunque rainforest and features views of the Caribbean Sea. Adjacent to the property is the Cabo Rojo Lighthouse, a historic lighthouse on the western tip of Cabo Rojo. Also nearby is Flamenco Beach, a beautiful white-sand beach located on the eastern tip of Cabo Rojo.

The resort property has a tropical climate with warm temperatures/high humidity. Average temperatures range from 75 degrees Fahrenheit (24 degrees Celsius) in winter vs. 85 degrees Fahrenheit (29 degrees Celsius) in summer. Peak season for the resort will be the coolest months of December through February.
