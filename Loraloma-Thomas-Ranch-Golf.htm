--- --- David McLay Kidd Golf in Austin, Texas

Land

Loraloma is the first development within Thomas Ranch to be built. Thomas Ranch is a 2,220-acre master-planned community in Spicewood, Texas about 30 miles west of Austin in the Texas Hill Country. Loraloma will offer 450 cottages, villas, estates, and custom homesites. The community address is 26400 SH 71 in Spicewood, Texas. The Thomas Ranch project is expected to take 7-10 years to complete with the broader community eventually having 3,500 or more homes, including single family, multi-family, senior and affordable housing.

The community will have a wide range of amenities:

  • David McLay Kidd golf course that is set to start construction this fall and open for play at the end of 2024
  • Swimming hole
  • Fishing pond
  • Pickleball courts
  • Clubhouse and dining
  • State-of-the-art wellness facility
  • Boutique hotel

    • David McLay Kidd Golf in Texas Hill Country

    The David McLay Kidd golf course being built at Loraloma is a 18 hole par-72 golf course that is planned to fit seamlessly into the natural terrain of the Texas Hill Country. The course will feature a wide variety of holes ranging from long, challenging par-4s to short, challenging risk-reward par-3 holes. The greens are anticipated to be large and undulating. The fairways are expected to be lined with native grasses and wildflowers, among them Texas bluebonnets. Kidd has designed over golf courses around the world, notably Bandon Dunes Golf Resort in Oregon and Nanea in Hawaii. The David Kidd course at Loraloma is expected to open in 2024. It will be a private course, but memberships will be available to residents of Loraloma and Thomas Ranch.

    Here are some additional details about the course:

  • The course will be located on 120 acres of land.
  • The course will have a total yardage of 7,200 yards from the back tees.
  • The course will have a slope rating of 140 and a course rating of 74.
  • The course will be irrigated with reclaimed water.
  • The course will be open to walkers and golfers who use carts.
