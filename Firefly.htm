Firefly - New Andrew Green Golf Course - Tennessee

Firefly is a new community and golf course planned in Tennessee. Thirty miles sout Nashville ills of the Appalachian Mountains, the property is 400+ acres. The community will feature 250 homes. Amenities include five miles of walk/bike trails.

Firefly Golf Course will be designed by golf architect Andrew Green. The 18-hole championship course will play 7,200 yards, par-72 from the back tees. Andrew Green will also design/build a short course to be included in the community's amenities. Facilities will include driving range with short game practice greens.

New golf courses in the Nashville area are growing. Bounty Club is expected to open in 2025.
