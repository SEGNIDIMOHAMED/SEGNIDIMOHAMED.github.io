Bounty Club - King Collins

The Bounty Club is set to be a national club and hallmark in King Collins' design career. Early views into the design near Nashville, Tennessee give anticipation for elite golf. Commitment to an environmentally sustainable approach integrating the natural topography of creeks and ravines gives the opportunity for a world class golf.

Sweetens Cove Golf Club in Tennessee may be the design duo's most famous creation to date. The project put Rob King and Tad Collins on the GCA map. Located in South Pittsburg, Tennessee, Sweetens Cove is a nine holer that opened in 2014 with wide fairways, dynamic green complexes, and tee grounds chosen by the golfers in the group, encouraging creative play and variety.

Landmand Golf Club is a large scale property set near the Nebraska/South Dakota/Iowa border on the Anderson family farm. The design features a fun downhill slinging redan green par 3 hole and a drivable par 4 with a Sitwell green replica at the 17th hole.
