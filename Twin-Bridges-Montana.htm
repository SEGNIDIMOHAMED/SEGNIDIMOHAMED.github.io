New Golf Course - Big Hole River - Twin Bridges

Twin Bridges, Montana is new site for a new golf course on the Big Hole River downstream from the Beaverhead Mountains area in Southwest Montana. 90 minutes from Bozeman or 2 hours to Yellowstone National Park, the area is centrally located. The Big Hole River is a well known for fly fishing.

Golf course construction is underway. Kye Goalby is leading design build efforts onsite. Design associates of Goalby onsite are Eamon Sullivan and Matt Smallwood. Sullivan and Smallwood also assisted Goalby build Zac Blair's Tree Farm in South Carolina.
