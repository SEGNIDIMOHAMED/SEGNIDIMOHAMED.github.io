Aivot Golf & Sports Management Private Limited is a new organization formed by Aivot CEO Alok Tiwari with a group of Indian and American investors to build a series of Indian golf courses with training centers. The new organziation has the support of The PGA of America. Golf course architect Tim Lobb is leading the design and implementation across multiple golf destinations across India.

This strategic collaboration aims to elevate golf in India through the creation of world-class golf courses, golf academies, and state-of-the-art hotel and residential amenities. The partnership was announced in 2023 at PGA of America headquarters in Frisco, Texas. David Blum from Aivot is a partner helping plan golf property development with Crozier Design Studios assisting. To date, named areas of focus include regions/cities: Mumbai, Pune, Goa, Delhi, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad, Chennai, Bangalore, Chandigarh, Lucknow

The island of Goa is considered one of the primary sites to build their first integrated golf resort. Goa is a small state on the southwestern coast of India.
