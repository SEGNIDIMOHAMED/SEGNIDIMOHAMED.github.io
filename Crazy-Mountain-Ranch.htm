Coore Crenshaw - Crazy Mountain Ranch in Montana

The new walkable 18-Hole Golf Course at Crazy Mountain Ranch designed by Bill Coore & Ben Crenshaw, will be exceptional in every regard. The golf course is anticipated to be a challenging, playable course that seamlessly blends with the landscape & takes full advantage of the exceptional mountain views, aspen groves & wide open spaces that surround the Ranch.

Bill Coore and World Golf Hall of Fame member Ben Crenshaw, the renowned architectural firm Coore & Crenshaw has been enlisted to create a Montana golf experience. Bill & Ben have a shared philosophy that traditional, strategic golf in a natural environment is the most rewarding, creative form of golf design.

Currently under construction, this design will provide members with a walkable 18 holes of inspired golf. Comfort stations and a halfway house will be placed conveniently on the course. A par 3 short course is also being planned. Fly fishing, skeet shooting and other activities are being planned as well over the 18,000 acre expanse.

The bedrock in the Crazy Mountains is formed by the Big Timber Stock, a large igneous intrusion. The stock is of the Tertiary age consisting of diorite and gabbro with zones of Quartz Monzodiorite which over time has been intruded by many dikes and sills.

Lone Mountain Land Company, LLC is a company formed by CrossHarbor Capital Partners to manage the planning, entitlement, building, marketing, and sale of premier real estate communities in and around Big Sky, Montana and other locations worldwide. Commitment to creating the ultimate Montana experience through thoughtful growth of their community is commendable.
