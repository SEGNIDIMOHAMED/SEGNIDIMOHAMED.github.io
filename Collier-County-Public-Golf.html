The Gate in Collier County - Public Golf

The Gate is a new golf course being designed by Peter Jacobsen and Jim Hardy. The site is formerly, Golden Gate, a closed golf course being redeveloped into 25 acres of affordable housing by Collier County. Part of the site is being proposed by Arthrex to serve as repurposed First Tee and 9 hole golf course public facility. The new 9 hole golf course would be designed by PGA Tour golfer Peter Jacobsen.

As part of the plan, the new "Willow Oak 9" golf course would highlight the natural beauty of the existing Willow Oak tree landscape. The Collier County area lacks opportunity and tee times for public golf.

In addition to the golf course, the plan includes a driving range and practice area for introduction to golf by The First Tee.

Peter Jacobsen and Jim Hardy founded Jacobsen Hardy Design and have a long history in golf course design. The team's portfolio includes Moody Gardens Golf Course, which offers a unique tropical golfing experience. The Wanderers Club at Wellington is another gem, known for its meticulously maintained fairways and greens. Brasada and Grays Crossing are also highly regarded, offering stunning views and challenging layouts. Other notable designs include Hammock Bay, Salishan, Moorpark CC, Redstone, Stone Creek, BlackHorse, Cypress Ridge, Creekside, and the prestigious Oregon Golf Club. Each of these courses showcases the duo's commitment to golf harmoniously coexisting with the natural environment.
