Tim Jackson and David Kahn have extensive experience in golf course design, having worked on both new golf coourses and renovation projects around the world. The team believes that a well-designed course should be fair to all skill levels, and that it should incorporate the natural beauty of its surroundings. Jackson Kahn Design is a member of the American Society of Golf Course Architects. Recent renovation projects include Convway Farms where the design team led Wadsworth construction in updating the Tom Fazio design.
