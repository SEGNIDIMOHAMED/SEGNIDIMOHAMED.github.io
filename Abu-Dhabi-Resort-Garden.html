Abu Dhabi Resort - Garden Course Renovation

Abu Dhabi Resort & Spa has hired golf course architect Peter Harradine re-design of the 9-hole Garden golf course.

Harradine designed the original course in 1998 and has updated his design view towards playability. The resort has hosted the Abu Dhabi HSBC Championship on the DP World Tour and the 2023 Eisenhower Trophy.

Construction is underway for a new routing with a back tee yardage of 2,755 yards Par 34. Late night golf will be available with stadium lighting on the fairways and greens.

Nearby Yas Links designed by Kyle Phillips has been internationally recognized in Top 100 golf course rankings for its design quality.
