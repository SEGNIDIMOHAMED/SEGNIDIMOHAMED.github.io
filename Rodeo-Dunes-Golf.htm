--- --- Rodeo Dunes - New Golf Course

Previewing New Dream Golf in Colorado

Rodeo Dunes is a new golf resort initiative announced in 2023. The golf courses are being constructed in the eastern plains of Colorado, near the town of Roggen. Roggen, Colorado is 42 miles from Denver International Airport (DIA) with a drive time is 45 minutes on Interstate 76 West. It is the brainchild of Michael and Chris Keiser, the sons of Bandon Dunes developer Mike Keiser. The land is over 2,000 acres. The soil type is characterized as sandy, ideal for golf. The first golf course is being designed by Jim Craig. Bill Coore and Ben Crenshaw will design subsequent golf courses.

Anticipation for the destination is high.

The property was previously owned by the Cervi family who operated a ranching farm on the land and ran the Cervi Championship Rodeo Company, the largest rodeo producer in the country. The name "Rodeo Dunes" is a nod to the property's history as a ranch, as well as the rolling, sandy terrain that resembles a rodeo arena.
