Punta Brava Golf & Surf Club is a Tom Doak led design project located in Baja California, Mexico near Ensenada. Doak is a well known golf course architect with a history in design of creating natural, playable courses with easy walkable transitions from hole to hole.

The property is located on the Punta Brava peninsula and will have dramatic oceanside carry shots from cape tees. Working onsite in agronomy oversight, design, shaping is CJ Kreuscher with Gerardo Rivera, Parker Anderson and Ben Malach. CJ Kreuscher was previously the superintendent at Tara Iti in New Zealand. Kreuscher also was on the maintenance and construction crew at Old Macdonald course in Bandon, Oregon.

Founding partners Brian Tucker, Hugo Salinas, and Ricardo Salinas hired Andrew Harris of Harris Kalinka creatively presents the animation for Punta Brava, highlighting the upcoming golf on the dramatic coastline tidal zone landscape.

The club is starting a new tradition. Aged over twenty-one years, Punta Brava Founders Reserve Tequila will be the founding member's legacy drink. Founding members include professional athletes Drew Brees, Steph Curry, Kelly Slater, and PGA Tour golfer Justin Thomas.

Also on the Punta Brava peninsula is La Bufadora. La Bufadora is a blowhole, a marine geyser up to 100 feet above the surface. La Bufadora is the 2nd largest marine geyser in the world. The best time to see the blowhole is during high tide, when the waves are strongest. The blowhole is also more active during the winter months.

Another nearby attraction are the vineyards and wineries in Valle de Guadalupe. This region is home to over 100 wineries providing ample opportunity to sample at wine tastings.
