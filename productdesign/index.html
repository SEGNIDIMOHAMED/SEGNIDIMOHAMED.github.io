aideM

The front of the phone handles the normal phone duties. Whilst the back of the phone is a multimedia centre handling movies, photos, music and file transfer…

The layout and shape of the phone was designed for easy operation and comfortable viewing of multimedia in both portrait and landscape orientations.

Formed our ‘sytems project,’ becoming a 3D model with functional buttons which controlled a navigable Power Point interface of the phones UI.

aideM featured in ‘Fiction Hotel,’ an exhibit at Kulturhuset, Stockholm, imagining a city in 2050.

Kalanda

An electronic calendar. One for unit for home and one for office, the units synchronise, once docked onto their stand, over a standard phoneline connection ensuring they are always fully up to date.

For example, amendments made to the organiser information at home by the wife, appear on the husbands unit at work.

The name Kalanda is a hybrid of calendar and LAN (Local Area Network), to highlight the connectivity of the device.

Kalanda was modeled in Alias Studiotools and put in situ using Adobe Photoshop.

Simpla

Simpla was an exercise in redesigning the humble stapler to require fewer components for more simple and cheaper manufacture.

Through clever use of materials and design, the stapler has a natural flex which does away with the need for a hinge section and thus reducing the number of components. The design has a strong resemblance to a hair grip or clip and this emphasises the notion of flexibility.

Constructed and rendered in Alias Studiotools.

Take>About

The cooler project was based on Igloo Coolers, a popular american brand of cooler.

Take>About is a lunchbox form factor for the school market. It features a two part form, the outer half provides the cool pack and sturdy protection, plus detachable shoulder strap. The draw fastens securely and can be removed for cleaning.

A model was produced in foam.

Saucer

Saucer was a solution to echoey public address(PA) announcements.

The dome shape and bottom fins difuse echoes away to nothing.

The unit contains conductive speakers for public address and background music whilst remaining sealed and waterproof from harsh conditions.

The top of the dome contains uplighting, power and hanging fixtures.

Produced for our ‘materials project,’ we were given materials with specific properties. Sound conduction in this case.

Model constructed in Alias Studiotools and put in situ with Adobe Photoshop.

Not affiliated with Tannoy Ltd. a UK PA system manufacturer.

Safe2O

Safe-2-O is a travel product for the average tourist to take away with them to purify the local water whilst on holiday. A brief dose from the germicidal lamp kills any harmful bacteria present in the local water. The product is designed to be much more travel friendly for the casual tourist to take away and keep them safe on their journey compared to more cubersome or techincal water purifying processes.

The device emits UV light in the UVC band, which destroys the DNA of any harmful bacteria rendering them effectively harmless.

Both views were constructed and rendered in Alias Studiotools with some Photoshop retouching.
