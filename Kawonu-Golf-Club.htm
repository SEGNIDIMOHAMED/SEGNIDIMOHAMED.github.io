Kawonu Golf Club - Andrew Green - South Carolina

Andrew Green will lead the design of Kawonu Golf Club in Greenville, South Carolina. Anticipated as a new opening in 2027, Green has partnered with Scott Ferrell and Barton Tuck to design a new golf course on the nearly 300 acre property. Ferrell is a South Carolina resident who led Gary Player Design for fifteen years as President, travelling to oversee new golf course designs globally.

The site is near Highway 418 and Fork Shoals Road. The golf course will be core golf with 2 whiskey loop alternative routings for shorter golf rounds as an option. The property is adjacent to the Reedy River and will be a par 72 measuring 7,325 yards from the back tees.

Andrew Green's 2025 calendar as a highly respected and sought-after golf course restoration/renovation expert remains full of high profile projects including recently reopened Interlachen Country Club in Minnesota and Karsten Creek in Oklahoma.
