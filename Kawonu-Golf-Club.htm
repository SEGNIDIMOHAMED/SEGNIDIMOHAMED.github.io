Kawonu Golf Club - Andrew Green - South Carolina

Andrew Green will lead the design of Kawonu Golf Club in Greenville, South Carolina. Anticipated as a new opening in 2027, Green has partnered with Scott Ferrell to design a new golf course on the nearly 300 acre property. Ferrell is a South Carolina resident who led Gary Player Design for fifteen years as President, travelling to oversee new golf course designs globally.

The site is near Highway 418 and Fork Shoals Road. The golf course will be core golf potentially integrating the Reedy River into the design.

Andrew Green's calendar as a highly respected and sought-after golf course restoration/renovation expert remains full of high profile projects at Interlachen Country Club in Minnesota and Karsten Creek in Oklahoma.
