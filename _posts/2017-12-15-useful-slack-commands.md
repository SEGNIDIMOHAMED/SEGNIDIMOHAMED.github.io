title: Useful Slack commands
---
| Hotkey | Function |
|---|---|
| ⌘ + T | Quick switcher. Jump through recent conversations or jump to a message thread. |
| ↑ | In message window, edit your last message. |
| : | In message window, starts searching for emojis. |
